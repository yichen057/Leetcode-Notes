# Behavioral Interview 行为面试

> Every company asks these. Prepare 5-6 STAR stories that cover all question categories. 提前写好故事，面试时只是"调取"。

## ⭐ STAR Framework

| Part | What | 时长占比 | 中文要点 |
|------|------|---------|---------|
| **S**ituation | Context: project, team, stakes | 15% | 一两句话交代背景，别啰嗦 |
| **T**ask | Your specific responsibility | 10% | 强调**你的**职责，不是团队的 |
| **A**ction | What YOU did, step by step | 60% | 重头戏！用 "I" 不用 "we"，讲决策和理由 |
| **R**esult | Quantified outcome + learning | 15% | 尽量带数字；加一句 takeaway |

**Timing**: 2-3 minutes per answer. 先一句话总结答案（"I'd say the time I... is a good example"），再展开 STAR。

## 🎯 High-Frequency Questions 高频题 & 答题角度

### Q1: Tell me about yourself.

**A (structure):** Present → Past → Future, 60-90 seconds. "I'm a CS student at X focusing on Y. Previously I interned at / built Z, where I [1-2 impressive specifics]. I'm excited about this role because [company-specific reason]."

**中文**: 不是自传，是**电梯演讲**：现在（学校/方向）→ 过去（最亮眼的 1-2 段经历，带数字）→ 未来（为什么是这家公司这个岗位）。提前背熟到自然。

---

### Q2: Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a teammate.

**A (angle):** Pick a professional disagreement (technical approach, prioritization), not a personality clash. Show you: listened to understand their view → found common ground in shared goals → resolved with data or a prototype → relationship stayed strong. Never badmouth the other person.

**中文**: 选**技术分歧**而不是人际矛盾。模板：先理解对方 → 数据/实验说话 → 以共同目标收尾。结果要体现"双赢 + 关系更好了"。

---

### Q3: Tell me about a time you failed.

**A (angle):** Choose a real failure with real stakes (not "I worked too hard"). Own it without blaming others, explain what you learned, and — crucially — show the changed behavior applied later. Interviewers grade the reflection, not the failure.

**中文**: 必须是**真失败**且你负主要责任。评分点在**反思和行为改变**："后来我在 X 项目就用了这个教训"。忌甩锅、忌假失败。

---

### Q4: Tell me about the most challenging project you've worked on.

**A (angle):** Pick the story with the most technical depth you can defend. Structure: why it was hard (unknowns, scale, constraints) → how you broke it down → key decisions and trade-offs → quantified result. Be ready for deep technical follow-ups.

**中文**: 选你**最能讲深**的项目（面试官一定追问细节）。难点要具体：性能瓶颈、未知领域、时间压力。准备好被问"为什么不用方案 B"。

---

### Q5: Tell me about a time you had to learn something quickly.

**A (angle):** Show a learning system, not just effort: identified the minimum needed → found the best resources (docs, experts, code) → built something small to validate → delivered. Quantify the time frame.

**中文**: 体现**学习方法论**：最小必要知识 → 官方文档/源码/请教专家 → 边做边学 → 按时交付。SDE intern 面试官最想看到这条。

---

### Q6: Why this company? / Why this role?

**A (angle):** Three-layer answer: product/mission (be specific — name a product decision or tech blog post you admired) → team/tech (what you'd work on) → personal fit (how your background maps). Generic answers are an instant red flag.

**中文**: 必须**公司定制**：提它具体的产品/技术博客/开源项目。三层：使命 → 技术 → 我能贡献什么。每家公司面前都要重写这题。

---

### Q7: Do you have any questions for me? (Reverse questions)

**A (good options):** "What does success look like for an intern in the first month?" / "What's the team's biggest technical challenge right now?" / "How does the team handle code review and mentorship?" — questions showing you're already thinking like a team member.

**中文**: 永远要提问（说没有 = 不感兴趣）。问成长、问团队技术挑战、问 mentorship。别问薪资福利（留给 HR 环节）。

## 🗣️ Delivery Tips 表达技巧

1. **"I" not "we"** — 面试官要评估的是你，行动部分必须用 I
2. **Numbers everywhere** — 提升了 30%、服务 1000 用户、提前 2 天交付
3. **Pause is fine** — 听到没准备的题，说 "Let me think for a second" 完全 OK
4. **Practice aloud** — 写出来 ≠ 说得出来，必须出声练 + 录音回听
5. **Amazon 特别注意** — 每道行为题对应 LP（见 [20-Amazon](../20-Amazon/)），答题时点出关键词
